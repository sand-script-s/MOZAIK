About
These works present some of my own explorations, using free association and collage technique, into realms of coincidence/synchronicity, and their potential as tools towards novel invention, insight and therapy.

Most of it was done by haphazardously stringing together a handful of videos based on some or other shared theme, object, pattern, or personal association, and then looking for myself to see what new coincidences (for example puns) arise sponaneously from that arrangement. I was often astounded by unexpected, thought-provoking and hilarious coincidences and as such I am sharing these in the hopes that others may be inspired thereby, and also try their own hand at various such methods. They may perhaps be quite surprised by what emerges unexpectedly from their own collages.
